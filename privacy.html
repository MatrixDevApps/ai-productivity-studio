1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Productivity Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our applications, websites, and services.

Key Principle: We only collect the information necessary to provide and improve our services, and we never sell your personal data to third parties.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details, preferences, and settings
  • Task and Productivity Data: Tasks, projects, notes, and other content you create within our apps
  • Communication Data: Messages when you contact our support team
  • Feedback and Surveys: Responses to surveys, feedback forms, and user research

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our apps, features used, and time spent
  • Performance Data: App crashes, response times, and technical diagnostics
  • Location Data: Approximate location (if enabled) for location-based reminders

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • App Store Data: Purchase information and reviews from app stores
  • Social Media: Profile information if you choose to connect social accounts
  • Integration Data: Information from third-party services you connect to our apps

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Provision

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our AI-powered productivity features
  • Process and fulfill your requests and transactions
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Sync your data across devices and platforms

3.2 AI and Machine Learning

  • Train and improve our AI algorithms for better task prioritization
  • Provide personalized recommendations and insights
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Develop new AI-powered features and capabilities

AI Processing: Your personal data is processed by our AI systems to provide intelligent features. This processing happens on secure servers with strict access controls.

3.3 Communication and Marketing

  • Send important service updates and security notifications
  • Provide product updates and feature announcements (with your consent)
  • Send promotional content and special offers (with your consent)
  • Conduct user research and surveys

3.4 Legal and Safety

  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Protect our rights, property, and safety
  • Investigate and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Enforce our terms of service and other agreements

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting and storage providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics and performance monitoring services
  • Customer support and communication platforms
  • Payment processing services (for premium subscriptions)

4.2 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

4.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

5.1 Encryption

  • Data encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Data encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data

5.2 Access Controls

  • Multi-factor authentication for all team members
  • Role-based access controls and principle of least privilege
  • Regular access reviews and audits

5.3 Infrastructure Security

  • Secure cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade security
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures and monitoring

Security Commitment: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We continuously monitor and improve our security practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill our legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after deletion
  • Usage Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 2 years
  • Support Communications: Retained for 3 years for quality assurance
  • Legal Compliance: Some data may be retained longer as required by law

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion feature in our apps.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Access your data through our apps and account settings

7.2 Correction and Updates

  • Update your account information and preferences
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Modify your privacy settings

7.3 Deletion and Restriction

  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request deletion of specific information
  • Restrict processing of your data

7.4 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Choose which updates to receive

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@aiproductivitystudio.com or use the settings in our apps.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with service providers
  • Adequacy decisions from relevant privacy authorities
  • Certification under recognized privacy frameworks
  • Your explicit consent where required

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

For users between 13 and 18, we recommend parental guidance and supervision when using our services.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Sending an email notification to your registered email address
  • Displaying a prominent notice in our apps
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Privacy Team
