Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with TaskWise AI? expand_more

Getting started with TaskWise AI is simple! Download the app from the App Store, create your account, and follow the onboarding process. The AI will learn your preferences and start providing intelligent task recommendations within the first few days of use.

Is my data secure and private? expand_more

Yes, absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all your data, and our AI processing happens on secure servers with strict privacy controls. We never sell your personal information, and you can delete your data at any time. Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I sync my tasks across multiple devices? expand_more

Yes! TaskWise AI automatically syncs your tasks, projects, and preferences across all your devices. Simply sign in with the same account on your iPhone, iPad, and Mac to access your productivity data anywhere.

How does the AI prioritization work? expand_more

Our AI analyzes your task patterns, deadlines, estimated time requirements, and historical completion data to intelligently prioritize your tasks. It learns from your behavior and adapts its recommendations to match your workflow preferences over time.

What if I need to cancel my subscription? expand_more

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. All your data will remain accessible, and you can reactivate anytime without losing your information.

Do you offer team or business plans? expand_more

Yes! We offer special business plans with team collaboration features, admin controls, and volume discounts. Contact our sales team at business@aiproductivitystudio.com for more information about enterprise solutions.
